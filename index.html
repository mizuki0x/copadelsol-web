Drop the gloves

Fighting is a mechanic, not a cutscene

Fighting is core to the game, not a scripted event. Square up, read your opponent, and settle it — a skill-based bout that runs on the same deterministic engine as every other second of the match.

01 · Square up

Instigate

Challenge a rival after a big hit. Both players drop the gloves and the bout begins.

02 · Wind up

Throw a punch

Every punch telegraphs before it lands. Commit too early and you're readable; wait too long and you're exposed.

03 · Read it

Guard the shot

Block at the right moment and the punch glances off. Get caught winding up and it lands clean.

04 · Payoff

Win the bout

Break their balance and they hit the ice. The winner earns a power play; the loser heads to the box.
