Privacy

Effective July 19, 2026

Free play does not require an account. Paid matchmaking uses the public wallet address you connect, signed one-time login challenges, opaque session identifiers, match inputs, replay digests, transaction IDs, timestamps, and operational error logs.

How data is used

Data is used to authenticate your wallet, pair opponents, run and verify matches, settle or refund escrow, prevent abuse, troubleshoot failures, and maintain public transaction receipts. A login signature is verified and discarded; it is not a transaction and cannot move funds. Session tokens are stored as one-way hashes by the service.

Public data

Wallet addresses, escrow state, payouts, refunds, and replay digests submitted to Base are public blockchain data and cannot be deleted by the site.

Retention and sharing

Expired login challenges and sessions are routinely deleted. Match records are retained for integrity, support, and dispute evidence. Infrastructure providers process service and database traffic. The site does not sell personal data.

Your choices

You can play free without a wallet, cancel matchmaking before pairing, disconnect your wallet, and clear browser session storage. For a data request, contact hello@bluelinegame.com with the relevant public wallet address. Never send a seed phrase or private key.
